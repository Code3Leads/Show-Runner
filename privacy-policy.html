Code 3 Leads Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

Code 3 Leads respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Information We Collect

When customers contact a business using the Code 3 Leads system, we may collect information such as:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Service address
  • Description of the service request

This information is collected only when voluntarily provided by the user during a phone call, text interaction, or form submission.

How We Use Information

The information collected through the Code 3 Leads system is used to:

  • Deliver service requests to contractors
  • Notify contractors of customer inquiries
  • Facilitate communication between customers and businesses
  • Improve the performance and reliability of our services

SMS Messaging & Communication

By contacting a business using the Code 3 Leads system, you consent to receive SMS messages related to your inquiry or service request. These messages are sent in response to customer-initiated contact and are not promotional.

Message frequency may vary depending on the interaction. Message and data rates may apply.

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. For assistance, reply HELP.

Data Sharing

Code 3 Leads does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

Information may only be shared with the intended business or contractor for the purpose of responding to your request.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information submitted through our system from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (such as messaging and communication providers) to operate our platform. These providers are used solely to facilitate service functionality and are not permitted to use your information for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

You may request to stop receiving communications at any time by replying STOP to any SMS message or by contacting us directly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
